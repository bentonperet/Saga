* **Utility Redundancy:** The switchyard design provides redundancy through dual utility feeds or other utility-side redundancy measures  
* **Acceptable Redundancy Model:** PGCIS assumes that utility-provided redundancy (via switchyard configuration) is the accepted backup power strategy for this facility  
* **Load Shedding Procedure:** In the event of a utility-wide outage or loss of both utility feeds, PGCIS may assist Hut 8 in developing and validating load shedding procedures to manage available capacity, prioritize critical loads, and execute graceful shutdowns as necessary  
* **UPS Battery Runtime:** UPS battery systems provide ride-through capacity for short-duration utility disturbances but are not intended for extended outage scenarios without utility restoration or load shedding
