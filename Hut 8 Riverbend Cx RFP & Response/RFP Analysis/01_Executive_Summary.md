## Project Overview

**Client:** Hut 8
**Project:** Riverbend Data Center - Louisiana
**Total Capacity:** 224 MW IT capacity across 6 data halls + 1 Network Room
**Utility Power:** 300 MW
**Phased Deployment:** 36 MW blocks

## Key Timeline

### Switchyard Commissioning
- **Duration:** May 2026 - July 2026 (3 months)
- **Scope:** Utility interconnection and switchyard systems

### Data Hall Commissioning
- **Duration:** August 2026 - February 2027 (7 months)
- **Phasing:** Six 36 MW blocks commissioned sequentially every 2 months
- **Level 4 IST Duration:** Maximum 6 weeks per 36 MW block

## Commissioning Standards

- **ASHRAE Guidelines** for data center commissioning
- **NEBB Standards** for testing and balancing
- **Level 1-5 Commissioning + IST** required

## Scope Responsibility Matrix

### Level 1-2: GC Performed, CxA Oversight
- Documentation and submittal review
- Factory testing verification
- Installation verification
- Physical installation QA

### Level 3: GC Performed, CxA Oversight
- Pre-functional testing
- Start-up procedures

### Level 4: CxA Full Execution
- Functional performance testing
- Heat load testing
- Electrical system level testing
- Mechanical system level testing

### Level 5 (IST): CxA Full Execution
- Integrated systems testing
- Full facility verification
- Complete documentation
- Systems integration validation

## Key Requirements

### Deliverables Required in Proposal
1. **Schedule Estimate:** Duration and sequencing by phase
2. **Manpower Plan:** Staffing requirements and duration by phase
3. **Cost Proposal:**
   - Level and phase-based breakdown
   - Equipment purchase/rental costs (separate line item)
   - QA/QC services (separate line item if not included in standard Cx)
4. **Relevant Experience:** Large data center and hyperscale facility commissioning

### CxA-Provided Equipment
- All commissioning test equipment to be supplied by CxA
- Any equipment that cannot be supplied must be called out in proposal
- Separate cost line items for equipment purchase/rental

### QA/QC Services
- Must be presented as separate service offering or independent line item
- Not to be bundled with standard commissioning unless clearly delineated

## Critical Constraints

1. **IST Duration Limit:** Maximum 6 weeks per 36 MW block for Level 4 IST
2. **Phased Overlapping Schedule:** Multiple data halls in commissioning simultaneously
3. **Fast-Track Timeline:** 2-month intervals between block energizations
4. **Scale:** 224 MW total capacity is hyperscale
5. **Standards Compliance:** Both ASHRAE and NEBB standards required

## Business Considerations

### Strengths of This Opportunity
- Large-scale project with clear phasing
- Hyperscale experience demonstration
- Multi-year engagement (May 2026 - Feb 2027+)
- Equipment list provided upfront
- Clear commissioning level definitions

### Challenges
- Aggressive IST timeline (6 weeks max per 36 MW block)
- Overlapping commissioning phases require significant staffing
- GC oversight responsibility for Levels 1-3 increases coordination needs
- Equipment list subject to change per final engineering
- White space configuration TBD (3 options provided)

### Differentiators to Emphasize
- Prior hyperscale data center commissioning experience
- ASHRAE and NEBB standard expertise
- Ability to manage overlapping phased deployments
- In-house testing equipment capabilities
- Integrated QA/QC methodology
