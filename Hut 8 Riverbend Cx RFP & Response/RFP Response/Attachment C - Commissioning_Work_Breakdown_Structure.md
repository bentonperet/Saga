## Detailed Weekly Deployment Schedule

### Planning Period (Weeks -16 & -15): April 12 - April 25, 2026

**Management & Coordination (50% On-Site Hybrid):**
- **Ben Solem (Cx Manager):** Program planning, commissioning plan development, vendor coordination strategy, safety planning, kick-off preparation
- **Lashica Woodard (Cx Scheduler/Coordinator):** Master schedule development, issue tracking system setup, performance dashboard configuration, meeting coordination framework

**Documentation (100% On-Site):**
- **Doc Control A:** Document management system setup, template development, submittal log creation, standards and procedures documentation

**Key Planning Activities:**
1. **Commissioning Plan Finalization:** Complete project-specific commissioning plan based on BOD and equipment lists
2. **Master Schedule Development:** Develop detailed commissioning schedule with critical path analysis
3. **Issue Tracking System:** Configure IRL (Issue Resolution Log) with tracking workflows and escalation procedures
4. **Performance Analytics Setup:** Build KPI dashboards for schedule variance, quality metrics, and issue trending
5. **Document Management:** Establish folder structures, naming conventions, and version control procedures
6. **Vendor Coordination:** Identify critical equipment vendors, schedule FAT witnessing, coordinate startup support
7. **Testing Equipment Planning:** Identify, specify, and procure/rent all required commissioning test equipment
8. **Safety Program:** Develop project-specific safety plan, coordinate with GC safety program, LOTO procedures
9. **Site Logistics:** Coordinate office space, communication systems, access control, parking
10. **Kick-off Preparation:** Prepare kick-off meeting materials, agendas, team introductions

**Strategic Note:** This 2-week planning period ensures all systems, procedures, and coordination are in place before field commissioning begins, maximizing efficiency and preventing delays during Phase 0 execution.

## Notes on Schedule Compliance

1. **Sunday-Based Calendar:** All weeks begin on Sunday to align with construction industry standard weekly reporting cycles.

2. **Phase 0 Negative Numbering:** Using negative week numbers (-14 to -1) clearly delineates Phase 0 from Phases 1-6 and maintains continuity with the August 2026 Week 1 reference point.

3. **6-Week L4-L5 Window:** Each phase has exactly 6 weeks for L4-L5 completion. This tight timeline requires exceptional L2-L3 quality to prevent delays.

4. **Team Mobility:** Lead MCx-A and MCx Tech-A transition from Team B back to Team A after Week 6 (Phase 1 completion) to support Team A's first L4-L5 rotation lead for Phase 2.

5. **Continuous L2-L3 Coverage:** While leads rotate for L4-L5, technicians maintain continuous L2-L3 coverage for upcoming phases, ensuring no gaps in quality oversight.

6. **Performance Tracking:** Lashica Woodard's Issue Tracking and Performance Analytics capabilities provide real-time visibility into program health, enabling proactive schedule risk management.
