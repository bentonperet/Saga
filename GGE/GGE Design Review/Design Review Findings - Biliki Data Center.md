## Executive Summary

This design review identifies **Critical, High, and Medium severity** issues in the Biliki Data Center design documents. The review focuses on Tier III compliance, capacity planning, system redundancy, and technical specifications that could impact facility reliability, operations, or certification.

**Key Findings:**
- **5 Critical Issues** - Immediate design changes required
- **8 High Issues** - Require resolution before construction
- **6 Medium Issues** - Should be addressed during detailed design
