# Senior Infrastructure Manager

**Location:** Tbilisi, Georgia

## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. This integrated approach positions GGE as a leader in sustainable digital infrastructure, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and operational excellence are not just compatible‚Äîthey're inseparable.

---

## Facility Introduction

Lead infrastructure excellence at Georgian Green Energy's premier Tbilisi facility, where mission-critical data center operations and sustainable hydroelectric generation demand world-class technical leadership. This integrated environment offers a unique opportunity to drive operational excellence across two critical infrastructure domains.

## Job Summary

The Senior Infrastructure Manager will lead all IT infrastructure operations for the Data Center, ensuring maximum reliability, performance, and efficiency of compute, storage, network, and virtualization platforms. This strategic leadership role manages technical teams, drives infrastructure strategy, and ensures delivery of exceptional service to data center customers.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** Chief Information Technology Officer (CITO)
- **Direct Reports:** Infrastructure Engineers, Network Engineers, System Administrators, Data Center Technicians
- **Key Relationships:** Critical Infrastructure Engineer, data center operations, CISO, SLA Team Lead, customers, technology vendors

## Roles & Responsibilities

**Strategic Leadership:**
- Develop and execute IT infrastructure strategy aligned with business objectives
- Define technology roadmap for compute, storage, network, and virtualization platforms
- Lead capacity planning and scalability initiatives
- Evaluate and recommend new technologies and architectures
- Provide technical leadership to CITO and executive team

**Infrastructure Operations:**
- Oversee 24/7/365 operation of all IT infrastructure systems
- Ensure achievement of customer SLA commitments (99.99%+ uptime)
- Manage multi-vendor server, storage, and network environments
- Optimize infrastructure performance, availability, and efficiency
- Lead incident response and root cause analysis for major incidents

**Virtualization & Cloud:**
- Manage enterprise virtualization platforms (VMware, Hyper-V, KVM)
- Oversee private cloud infrastructure and orchestration
- Integrate hybrid cloud capabilities (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Implement infrastructure-as-code and automation strategies
- Optimize virtual machine density and resource utilization

**Network Infrastructure:**
- Lead network architecture and design for data center and enterprise
- Manage core, distribution, and access layer switching and routing
- Oversee network security (firewalls, IPS/IDS, segmentation)
- Implement SDN (Software-Defined Networking) where appropriate
- Ensure network performance, redundancy, and scalability

**Storage Systems:**
- Manage enterprise storage platforms (SAN, NAS, object storage)
- Implement backup, replication, and disaster recovery solutions
- Optimize storage performance and capacity utilization
- Ensure data protection and business continuity capabilities
- Evaluate emerging storage technologies (NVMe, all-flash, cloud storage)

**Data Center Services:**
- Oversee colocation, hosting, and managed services delivery
- Support customer onboarding and technical requirements
- Manage customer-facing portal and self-service capabilities
- Coordinate with SLA Team on service delivery and customer satisfaction
- Define and price new infrastructure service offerings

**Team Leadership:**
- Build, mentor, and develop high-performing infrastructure team
- Establish technical standards, procedures, and best practices
- Foster culture of operational excellence and continuous improvement
- Conduct performance reviews and career development planning
- Ensure team training and professional development

**Vendor & Technology Management:**
- Manage relationships with major technology vendors (Dell, HP, Cisco, VMware, etc.)
- Negotiate contracts, licensing, and support agreements
- Coordinate vendor escalations and executive engagement
- Evaluate proof-of-concepts and emerging technologies
- Manage infrastructure procurement and lifecycle

**Operational Excellence:**
- Implement ITIL service management processes
- Drive automation and orchestration initiatives
- Establish monitoring, alerting, and performance dashboards
- Lead change management and release processes
- Conduct capacity reviews and infrastructure optimization

**Security & Compliance:**
- Collaborate with CISO on infrastructure security posture
- Implement security hardening and patch management
- Ensure compliance with customer requirements and certifications
- Coordinate security assessments and penetration testing
- Manage privileged access and audit logging

**Business Continuity:**
- Develop and test disaster recovery and business continuity plans
- Ensure infrastructure resiliency and redundancy
- Coordinate DR testing and failover exercises
- Manage backup and recovery operations
- Plan and execute infrastructure migrations

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or related field
- Minimum 10 years in IT infrastructure roles
- At least 5 years in leadership or management positions
- Extensive experience with enterprise virtualization (VMware VCP required)
- Expert knowledge of networking (Cisco CCNP or equivalent highly valued)
- Strong understanding of storage technologies (SAN, NAS, backup/DR)
- Experience managing data center operations or colocation services
- Excellent command of English and Georgian
- Proven ability to manage large teams and multi-million dollar budgets

## Desired Qualifications

- Master's degree or MBA
- Advanced certifications (CCIE, VCDX, RHCA, or equivalent)
- Experience with Tier III/IV data center operations
- Background in cloud platforms (AWS Solutions Architect, Azure Administrator)
- Knowledge of infrastructure automation (Ansible, Terraform, Kubernetes)
- Experience with ITIL and ITSM platforms (ServiceNow, Remedy)
- Understanding of data center physical infrastructure
- Previous P&L responsibility for infrastructure services
- Project management certification (PMP, PRINCE2)
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Senior Management** (10+ years experience, 5+ years leadership)
