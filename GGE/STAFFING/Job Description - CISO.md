# CISO - Chief Information Security Officer

**Location:** Tbilisi, Georgia

## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. This integrated approach positions GGE as a leader in sustainable digital infrastructure, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and operational excellence are not just compatible‚Äîthey're inseparable.

## Facility Introduction

Protect critical infrastructure at Georgian Green Energy's integrated facility in Tbilisi, where cybersecurity isn't just about data protection‚Äîit's about safeguarding essential energy and digital services. Our state-of-the-art data center and hydroelectric plant require world-class security leadership to defend against evolving cyber threats while maintaining operational continuity.

## Job Summary

The Chief Information Security Officer will lead all aspects of cybersecurity strategy and execution across both the Data Center and Hydro Power Plant operations. This mission-critical role ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information systems while protecting industrial control systems (ICS) and operational technology (OT) from cyber threats.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- **Direct Reports:** OT Security Lead, IT Security Team, Security Operations Center (SOC) staff, Compliance & Audit staff
- **Key Relationships:** CITO, Critical Infrastructure Engineer, HPP engineering leadership, data center clients, regulatory authorities

## Roles & Responsibilities

**Security Strategy & Governance:**
- Develop and implement comprehensive information security strategy for IT and OT environments
- Establish security policies, standards, and procedures aligned with ISO 27001, NIST, and IEC 62443 frameworks
- Lead risk assessment and threat modeling for both data center and power plant operations
- Report security posture and incidents to CEO and Board of Directors

**Operational Security Management:**
- Oversee 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) monitoring both IT and OT networks
- Manage incident response, forensics, and recovery operations
- Implement and maintain security information and event management (SIEM) systems
- Conduct regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

**OT/ICS Security:**
- Secure SCADA systems, PLCs, and industrial control networks at the hydro power plant
- Implement network segmentation and zone/conduit architecture for critical systems
- Oversee OT Security Lead in protecting hydroelectric generation and distribution systems
- Coordinate with HPP engineering on secure remote access and maintenance procedures

**Data Center Security:**
- Ensure security of customer data and multi-tenant environments
- Manage access controls, identity management, and privileged account security
- Oversee physical security integration with cybersecurity measures
- Maintain compliance with customer SLAs and security requirements (SOC 2, PCI-DSS where applicable)

**Compliance & Risk Management:**
- Ensure compliance with Georgian data protection laws and EU GDPR where applicable
- Manage cybersecurity insurance and risk transfer mechanisms
- Coordinate external audits and certification processes
- Develop and maintain business continuity and disaster recovery plans from security perspective

**Team Leadership & Culture:**
- Build and mentor high-performing security team across IT and OT domains
- Establish security awareness training programs for all staff
- Foster culture of security-first thinking across the organization
- Collaborate with CITO on secure architecture and technology selection

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Security, or related field
- Minimum 12 years in information security roles
- At least 5 years in senior security leadership positions
- Expert knowledge of IT security technologies (firewalls, IDS/IPS, SIEM, EDR)
- Strong understanding of OT/ICS security principles and standards (IEC 62443, NERC CIP)
- Experience securing critical infrastructure (energy, utilities, or data centers)
- Professional security certifications (CISSP, CISM required; GICSP highly valued)
- Excellent command of English and Georgian

## Desired Qualifications

- Advanced security certifications (CISA, CRISC, GIAC certifications, CEH)
- Experience with SCADA security and industrial control systems
- Background in security operations center (SOC) management
- Knowledge of European cybersecurity directives (NIS2, CER)
- Experience with cloud security and multi-tenant environments
- Incident response and forensics expertise
- Previous role securing power generation or distribution facilities
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Senior Executive** (12+ years experience, 5+ years leadership)
