## Facility Introduction

Drive customer excellence at Georgian Green Energy's Tbilisi Data Center, where world-class service delivery and customer satisfaction are paramount. This state-of-the-art facility serves demanding enterprise customers who expect exceptional reliability, responsiveness, and technical expertise.

## Job Summary

The SLA Team Lead will manage all aspects of service level agreement (SLA) compliance, customer relationship management, and service delivery excellence for data center customers. This customer-facing leadership role ensures that technical services consistently meet or exceed commitments while fostering strong customer partnerships.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** CITO or Operations Director
- **Direct Reports:** SLA Team members, Customer Support Specialists
- **Key Relationships:** Senior Infrastructure Manager, data center operations, customers, sales/account management, vendors

## Roles & Responsibilities

**SLA Management:**
- Monitor and ensure compliance with all customer SLA commitments
- Track uptime, performance metrics, and service level indicators
- Generate SLA reports and performance dashboards
- Conduct monthly/quarterly SLA reviews with customers
- Manage SLA credits and remediation for any breaches
- Continuously improve SLA attainment and service reliability

**Customer Relationship Management:**
- Serve as primary technical point of contact for key customers
- Build and maintain strong customer relationships and trust
- Conduct regular business reviews and customer meetings
- Understand customer requirements, roadmaps, and strategic initiatives
- Proactively communicate service impacts, maintenance windows, and changes
- Escalate and resolve customer issues and concerns

**Incident & Problem Management:**
- Lead major incident response for customer-impacting events
- Coordinate cross-functional teams during service disruptions
- Provide timely customer communication during incidents
- Conduct post-incident reviews and root cause analysis
- Implement preventive measures to avoid recurrence
- Track problem tickets to resolution

**Service Delivery:**
- Oversee provisioning of new customer services and deployments
- Coordinate implementation projects and customer onboarding
- Manage change requests and service modifications
- Ensure quality of service delivery and installation work
- Monitor customer portal and ticketing system
- Drive continuous service improvement initiatives

**Team Leadership:**
- Lead and develop SLA team members
- Establish team processes, procedures, and standards
- Conduct training on customer service excellence
- Foster customer-centric culture and mindset
- Perform team performance reviews and coaching
- Ensure 24/7 coverage and on-call rotation

**Performance Monitoring:**
- Implement comprehensive monitoring and alerting for customer services
- Track infrastructure performance, capacity, and utilization
- Analyze trends and proactively address potential issues
- Generate customer-facing performance reports
- Monitor network latency, packet loss, and throughput
- Ensure monitoring tools accuracy and effectiveness

**Process Improvement:**
- Implement ITIL service management best practices
- Optimize ticketing workflows and escalation procedures
- Develop and maintain service catalogs and documentation
- Create knowledge base articles and troubleshooting guides
- Measure and improve key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Conduct customer satisfaction surveys and act on feedback

**Vendor Coordination:**
- Manage third-party vendors supporting customer services
- Coordinate vendor escalations and service requests
- Monitor vendor SLAs and performance
- Facilitate vendor access and coordination with customers
- Ensure vendor compliance with security and quality standards

**Capacity Planning:**
- Monitor customer resource utilization and growth trends
- Coordinate with infrastructure team on capacity expansions
- Plan for seasonal peaks and anticipated growth
- Advise customers on capacity optimization
- Forecast infrastructure requirements based on customer pipelines

**Documentation & Reporting:**
- Maintain customer service documentation and runbooks
- Generate monthly service reports and executive summaries
- Track and report on service metrics and KPIs
- Document customer configurations and special requirements
- Create standard operating procedures for service delivery

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Business, or related field
- Minimum 7 years experience in IT service delivery or operations
- At least 3 years in customer-facing or SLA management role
- Strong understanding of data center services (colocation, hosting, cloud)
- Experience with ITIL service management frameworks
- Excellent customer service and communication skills
- Proven ability to manage multiple customer relationships
- Excellent command of English and Georgian
- Experience with ticketing systems and monitoring platforms

## Desired Qualifications

- ITIL Foundation or higher certification
- Experience managing data center SLAs and uptime commitments
- Background in technical account management or customer success
- Knowledge of networking, virtualization, and cloud technologies
- Project management experience and certification
- Experience with ServiceNow, Remedy, or similar ITSM platforms
- Understanding of data center infrastructure and operations
- Previous team leadership experience
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Mid to Senior Professional** (7+ years experience, 3+ years customer-facing)
