# Training Coordinator

**Location:** Tbilisi, Georgia

## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. This integrated approach positions GGE as a leader in sustainable digital infrastructure, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and operational excellence are not just compatible‚Äîthey're inseparable.

## Facility Introduction

Develop excellence at Georgian Green Energy's Tbilisi facilities, where continuous learning and development ensure world-class operations across our data center and hydroelectric power plant. This critical role builds the competency and capability of technical teams operating mission-critical infrastructure.

## Job Summary

The Training Coordinator will develop, implement, and manage comprehensive training programs for all personnel across both the Data Center and Hydro Power Plant operations. This essential role ensures that all staff have the knowledge, skills, and competencies required to safely and effectively perform their duties in these mission-critical environments.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** HR Director, Plant Manager, or Chief Operating Officer
- **Key Relationships:** All department heads, HSEQ Officer, HPP engineers, external training providers, regulatory authorities, equipment vendors

## Roles & Responsibilities

**Training Program Development:**
- Assess training needs across all departments and roles
- Develop comprehensive training curricula for technical and operational positions
- Create training materials, presentations, and hands-on exercises
- Design onboarding programs for new employees
- Develop specialized programs for data center and power plant operations
- Establish competency assessment and certification processes

**Technical Training Delivery:**
- Conduct technical training sessions for operations and maintenance personnel
- Deliver plant systems training (electrical, mechanical, SCADA, control systems)
- Provide data center infrastructure training (UPS, cooling, power distribution)
- Train on specialized equipment and manufacturer-specific systems
- Conduct simulator-based training for plant operators
- Facilitate hands-on skill development and practice sessions

**Compliance & Regulatory Training:**
- Ensure compliance with mandatory regulatory training requirements
- Coordinate occupational health and safety training with HSEQ Officer
- Deliver electrical safety, lockout/tagout, and confined space training
- Provide environmental compliance and permit training
- Arrange first aid, CPR, and emergency response certifications
- Track and maintain regulatory training records and certifications

**Vendor & External Training Coordination:**
- Coordinate manufacturer training on new equipment and systems
- Arrange external training courses and certifications
- Manage relationships with training providers and technical schools
- Schedule vendor-led training during equipment commissioning
- Coordinate professional development opportunities
- Facilitate industry conference and seminar attendance

**Learning Management:**
- Implement and manage learning management system (LMS)
- Track training completion, certifications, and qualifications
- Maintain training records and documentation for all employees
- Generate training reports and compliance dashboards
- Ensure training database accuracy and completeness
- Support audit and certification training requirements

**Competency Management:**
- Develop competency matrices for each role and position
- Conduct skills assessments and gap analysis
- Create individual development plans for employees
- Track qualification status and recertification requirements
- Manage operator qualification and authorization programs
- Ensure role-based competency requirements are met

**Onboarding & Orientation:**
- Design and deliver comprehensive new employee orientation
- Provide site-specific safety and security inductions
- Coordinate department-specific onboarding training
- Ensure new hires receive required initial training
- Track and report on onboarding completion
- Continuously improve orientation programs based on feedback

**Emergency Response Training:**
- Develop and conduct emergency response drills and exercises
- Train emergency response teams and first responders
- Conduct evacuation drills and crisis management exercises
- Provide incident command and emergency coordination training
- Coordinate fire safety and emergency equipment training
- Facilitate tabletop exercises for various emergency scenarios

**Continuous Improvement:**
- Evaluate training effectiveness through assessments and feedback
- Update training materials to reflect operational changes
- Incorporate lessons learned from incidents into training
- Benchmark against industry best practices
- Measure training impact on operational performance
- Innovate with new training methods and technologies

**Documentation & Reporting:**
- Maintain comprehensive training documentation and curricula
- Generate training reports for management and regulators
- Document training attendance and completion
- Create training calendars and schedules
- Prepare training budgets and track expenses
- Support certification audits with training evidence

**Cross-Functional Collaboration:**
- Work with department heads to identify training priorities
- Collaborate with HSEQ Officer on safety training initiatives
- Support engineering teams with technical skills development
- Coordinate with HR on performance and development
- Partner with vendors on product and equipment training
- Engage external subject matter experts as needed

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Education, Training & Development, Engineering, or related field
- Minimum 5 years experience in technical training or adult education
- Experience developing training programs for industrial or technical environments
- Strong presentation and facilitation skills
- Excellent instructional design and curriculum development abilities
- Proficiency with learning management systems (LMS)
- Excellent command of English and Georgian
- Knowledge of occupational health and safety training requirements
- Strong organizational and project management skills

## Desired Qualifications

- Professional trainer certification (ATD, CPLP, or equivalent)
- Experience in power generation or data center training
- Background in engineering or technical operations
- Knowledge of competency-based training and assessment
- Familiarity with simulator-based training for operations
- Experience with e-learning development tools
- Understanding of OSHA, ANSI, and international safety training standards
- Previous experience in utilities, critical infrastructure, or industrial facilities
- Instructional systems design (ISD) or ADDIE methodology experience
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Mid to Senior Professional** (5+ years experience)
