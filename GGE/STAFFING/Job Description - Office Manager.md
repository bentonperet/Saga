Keep operations running smoothly at Georgian Green Energy's integrated Tbilisi facility, where exceptional administrative leadership supports world-class technical teams. ## Job Summary

The Office Manager will oversee all administrative functions, office operations, and facilities management for the GGE complex. This critical support role ensures efficient daily operations, coordinates cross-functional activities, and provides executive-level administrative support to facility leadership.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** Plant Manager, Chief Operating Officer, or Facilities Director
- **Direct Reports:** Office Specialist, Reception/Administrative Assistant
- **Key Relationships:** All department heads, HR, finance, procurement, vendors, contractors, visitors

## Roles & Responsibilities

**Office Operations Management:**
- Oversee day-to-day administrative operations for office and administrative facilities
- Manage office supplies, equipment, and inventory procurement
- Coordinate office space planning, layout, and workspace assignments
- Ensure office areas are clean, organized, and professionally maintained
- Manage vendor relationships for office services (cleaning, catering, supplies)

**Executive & Administrative Support:**
- Provide high-level administrative support to Plant Manager and executive team
- Manage executive calendars, scheduling, and meeting coordination
- Prepare correspondence, reports, and presentations
- Organize and coordinate management meetings, site visits, and events
- Handle confidential information with discretion and professionalism

**Facilities Coordination:**
- Coordinate with maintenance teams on office and administrative building needs
- Manage access control, badging, and visitor management systems
- Oversee conference room bookings and AV equipment
- Coordinate office moves, renovations, and space modifications
- Ensure compliance with health, safety, and security protocols in office areas

**Communication & Coordination:**
- Serve as primary point of contact for administrative inquiries
- Coordinate communication between departments and facilities
- Distribute internal communications and announcements
- Manage bulletin boards, signage, and informational displays
- Support internal events, celebrations, and employee engagement activities

**Vendor & Contractor Management:**
- Coordinate contractor access and site orientation for office areas
- Manage service agreements for office equipment and systems
- Obtain quotes and proposals for administrative services
- Monitor vendor performance and service quality
- Process invoices and maintain vendor records

**Records & Documentation:**
- Maintain organized filing systems (physical and electronic)
- Manage document retention and archiving procedures
- Coordinate records management and confidential document disposal
- Ensure compliance with document control procedures
- Support audit and certification activities with documentation

**Travel & Logistics:**
- Coordinate travel arrangements for staff and visitors
- Manage accommodation bookings for site visitors and contractors
- Arrange ground transportation and logistics
- Process travel expense reports and reimbursements
- Maintain relationships with hotels, car services, and travel providers

**HR & Administrative Support:**
- Coordinate new employee onboarding and orientation logistics
- Manage employee records and personnel files (in coordination with HR)
- Track leave calendars and coordinate coverage
- Support performance review and training documentation
- Coordinate employee benefits administration
