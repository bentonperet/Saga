# CITO - Chief Information Technology Officer

**Location:** Tbilisi, Georgia

## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. This integrated approach positions GGE as a leader in sustainable digital infrastructure, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and operational excellence are not just compatible‚Äîthey're inseparable.

## Facility Introduction

Drive digital innovation at Georgian Green Energy's pioneering Tbilisi facility, where cutting-edge IT infrastructure powers both a world-class data center and a modern hydroelectric power plant. This unique dual-mission environment demands visionary technology leadership to deliver exceptional reliability and operational excellence.

## Job Summary

The Chief Information Technology Officer will lead all IT strategy, architecture, and operations across GGE's Data Center and Hydro Power Plant. This critical executive role ensures that technology infrastructure delivers maximum uptime, efficiency, and value while supporting business growth and digital transformation initiatives.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- **Direct Reports:** Senior Infrastructure Manager, IT Operations Team, Network Engineers, Data Center Operations, System Administrators, Help Desk/Support
- **Key Relationships:** CISO, HPP engineering leadership, Critical Infrastructure Engineer, data center clients, vendors and technology partners

## Roles & Responsibilities

**IT Strategy & Leadership:**
- Develop and execute comprehensive IT strategy aligned with business objectives
- Define technology roadmap for data center services and internal IT systems
- Lead digital transformation initiatives to enhance operational efficiency
- Provide technology guidance to executive team and Board of Directors

**Data Center Operations:**
- Oversee operation of Tier III/IV data center infrastructure
- Ensure achievement of SLA commitments (99.99%+ uptime targets)
- Manage colocation, hosting, and cloud service delivery
- Optimize data center capacity planning and resource utilization
- Coordinate with SLA Team Lead to maintain service excellence

**Infrastructure Management:**
- Direct management of compute, storage, and network infrastructure
- Oversee virtualization platforms (VMware, Hyper-V, KVM) and cloud integration
- Manage enterprise networking (core, distribution, and access layers)
- Ensure redundancy and high availability across all critical systems

**HPP IT Systems:**
- Support IT requirements for hydroelectric plant operations
- Manage integration between IT systems and OT/SCADA environments
- Ensure secure remote monitoring and control capabilities
- Coordinate with HPP engineering on technology upgrades

**Operational Excellence:**
- Establish IT service management (ITSM) processes based on ITIL framework
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and performance management systems
- Drive automation and orchestration initiatives
- Manage vendor relationships and technology procurement

**Team Development:**
- Build and lead high-performing IT operations team
- Establish training and professional development programs
- Foster culture of innovation and continuous improvement
- Collaborate with CISO on security-integrated technology solutions

**Business Development Support:**
- Work with sales/business development on technical proposals for data center clients
- Define new service offerings and capabilities
- Support customer technical requirements and escalations
- Ensure competitive positioning of technology capabilities

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or related field
- Minimum 12 years in IT leadership roles
- At least 5 years in senior technology management positions
- Extensive experience managing data center operations (colocation or cloud services)
- Expert knowledge of virtualization, storage, and networking technologies
- Strong understanding of ITIL, ITSM, and operational best practices
- Experience with both IT and OT/industrial environments
- Excellent command of English and Georgian
- Proven track record managing large IT budgets and teams

## Desired Qualifications

- Master's degree or MBA in technology-related field
- Professional certifications (ITIL Expert, VCP-DCV, CCNP, AWS/Azure certifications)
- Data center design and construction experience
- Experience with Tier III/IV certified data centers
- Background in critical infrastructure or utilities IT
- Knowledge of power plant monitoring and control systems
- Experience with hyperscale cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Project management certification (PMP, PRINCE2)
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Senior Executive** (12+ years experience, 5+ years leadership)
