## Facility Introduction

Champion safety and excellence at Georgian Green Energy's integrated Tbilisi facility, where a state-of-the-art data center and hydroelectric power plant operate under the highest standards of health, safety, environmental stewardship, and quality management. Every team member returns home safely because of the critical work you'll lead.

## Job Summary

The HSEQ Officer will develop, implement, and maintain comprehensive health, safety, environmental, and quality management systems across both the Data Center and Hydro Power Plant operations. This vital role creates and sustains a culture of zero incidents while ensuring regulatory compliance and continuous improvement in all aspects of HSEQ performance.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** Chief Operating Officer or Plant Manager
- **Direct Reports:** May supervise Safety Coordinators or HSEQ Specialists
- **Key Relationships:** All department heads, Plant Operators, Maintenance Technicians, Training Coordinator, regulatory authorities, contractors

## Roles & Responsibilities

**Safety Management:**
- Develop and implement comprehensive health and safety programs for both facilities
- Conduct regular safety inspections, audits, and risk assessments
- Investigate all incidents, near-misses, and unsafe conditions; implement corrective actions
- Maintain injury/illness records and safety performance metrics (TRIR, DART, LTIR)
- Manage lockout/tagout (LOTO), confined space, working at heights, and electrical safety programs
- Oversee personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements and compliance
- Lead emergency response planning and evacuation drills

**Environmental Compliance:**
- Ensure compliance with environmental permits and regulations for both facilities
- Monitor water quality, discharge permits, and environmental impact at HPP
- Oversee waste management, hazardous materials handling, and spill prevention
- Track environmental performance metrics (water usage, emissions, waste diversion)
- Coordinate environmental reporting to regulatory authorities
- Support sustainability initiatives and environmental improvement projects

**Quality Management:**
- Develop and maintain ISO 9001 quality management system (or equivalent)
- Conduct internal quality audits and management reviews
- Lead non-conformance investigations and corrective/preventive actions (CAPA)
- Support data center quality certifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2, etc.)
- Ensure quality of maintenance work and contractor services
- Drive continuous improvement initiatives using Lean/Six Sigma methodologies

**Training & Competency:**
- Collaborate with Training Coordinator on safety training curriculum
- Deliver HSEQ inductions for new employees and contractors
- Ensure regulatory training compliance (first aid, fire safety, electrical safety)
- Maintain training records and competency assessments
- Promote safety awareness campaigns and behavioral safety programs

**Contractor Management:**
- Develop contractor HSEQ requirements and pre-qualification process
- Conduct contractor safety orientations and work permit management
- Monitor contractor safety performance and compliance
- Coordinate contractor emergency response integration

**Compliance & Reporting:**
- Ensure compliance with Georgian labor, safety, and environmental laws
- Manage relationships with regulatory inspectors and authorities
- Prepare HSEQ reports for management and regulatory submissions
- Maintain HSEQ documentation, procedures, and records
- Coordinate external HSEQ audits and certifications

**Culture & Leadership:**
- Foster proactive safety culture and employee engagement
- Lead safety committee meetings and toolbox talks
- Recognize and reward positive HSEQ behaviors
- Champion "stop work authority" and speak-up culture
- Promote environmental stewardship and sustainability mindset

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Occupational Health & Safety, Environmental Science, Engineering, or related field
- Minimum 5 years experience in HSEQ roles in industrial or critical infrastructure settings
- Strong knowledge of Georgian health, safety, and environmental regulations
- Experience with ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 management systems
- Professional safety certification (NEBOSH, IOSH, or equivalent)
- Excellent command of English and Georgian
- Strong communication and training delivery skills
- Proficiency with HSEQ management software and incident tracking systems

## Desired Qualifications

- Professional certifications (CSP, CIH, CRSP, or international equivalents)
- Experience in power generation or data center environments
- Background in environmental compliance and permitting
- Knowledge of OSHA standards and international best practices
- Internal auditor certifications for ISO standards
- Lean Six Sigma Green Belt or higher
- Experience with behavior-based safety programs
- Emergency response or rescue team experience
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Mid to Senior Professional** (5+ years experience)
