# Office Specialist

**Location:** Tbilisi, Georgia

## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. This integrated approach positions GGE as a leader in sustainable digital infrastructure, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and operational excellence are not just compatible‚Äîthey're inseparable.

## Facility Introduction

Support daily operations at Georgian Green Energy's innovative Tbilisi facility, where a state-of-the-art data center and hydroelectric power plant rely on efficient administrative support. Your organizational skills and attention to detail will help maintain the professional environment our technical teams depend on.

## Job Summary

The Office Specialist will provide comprehensive administrative support across all departments, handling reception duties, documentation, scheduling, and general office coordination. This important role ensures smooth daily operations and excellent customer service for both internal staff and external visitors.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** Office Manager
- **Key Relationships:** All departments, vendors, contractors, visitors, customers

## Roles & Responsibilities

**Reception & Visitor Management:**
- Greet and assist visitors, contractors, and customers professionally
- Manage visitor check-in, badging, and security procedures
- Coordinate visitor escorts and site tours
- Answer and route incoming phone calls and emails
- Maintain professional reception area appearance

**Administrative Support:**
- Provide administrative assistance to various departments as needed
- Prepare documents, reports, and correspondence
- Manage filing, scanning, and document organization
- Coordinate meeting room bookings and setup
- Arrange catering and refreshments for meetings and events

**Office Coordination:**
- Manage office supplies inventory and ordering
- Coordinate mail, courier, and package handling
- Distribute incoming correspondence and deliveries
- Maintain office equipment (printers, copiers, phones)
- Support office organization and cleanliness

**Scheduling & Calendar Support:**
- Coordinate meeting schedules and calendar management
- Arrange travel and accommodation bookings
- Schedule facility tours and contractor access
- Maintain shared calendars and conference room schedules
- Send meeting reminders and distribute agendas

**Documentation & Records:**
- Maintain filing systems and document organization
- Process forms, applications, and administrative paperwork
- Create and update spreadsheets and databases
- Photocopy, scan, and distribute documents
- Assist with record retention and archiving

**Communication:**
- Handle general inquiries via phone, email, and in-person
- Route requests to appropriate departments
- Maintain contact lists and distribution groups
- Support internal communications and announcements
- Translate documents between English and Georgian as needed

**Support Functions:**
- Assist with onboarding new employees (workspace setup, supplies)
- Coordinate contractor site access and orientation
- Support HR with administrative tasks and documentation
- Assist with event planning and employee activities
- Provide backup support for other administrative functions

## Required Qualifications

- High school diploma or equivalent; associate's or bachelor's degree preferred
- Minimum 2 years administrative or office support experience
- Strong organizational and time management skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook)
- Professional appearance and customer service orientation
- Good command of English and Georgian
- Ability to multitask and handle competing priorities
- Friendly, helpful, and professional demeanor

## Desired Qualifications

- Previous experience in technical, industrial, or corporate office environment
- Familiarity with visitor management or access control systems
- Experience with calendar management and meeting coordination
- Basic knowledge of office equipment troubleshooting
- Receptionist or customer service training
- Bilingual fluency in English and Georgian
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Entry to Mid-Level Professional** (2+ years experience)
