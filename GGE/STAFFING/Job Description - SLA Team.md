## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. ## Facility Introduction

Deliver exceptional service at Georgian Green Energy's Tbilisi Data Center, where customer success drives everything we do. Join a team dedicated to ensuring world-class uptime, performance, and customer satisfaction in a state-of-the-art colocation and hosting environment.

## Job Summary

SLA Team members will monitor service delivery, ensure SLA compliance, and provide technical support to data center customers. This customer-focused role combines technical expertise with excellent communication skills to maintain service excellence and build strong customer relationships.

## Reporting Structure

- **Reports to:** SLA Team Lead
- **Key Relationships:** Data center operations, infrastructure teams, customers, vendors

## Roles & Responsibilities

**SLA Monitoring:**
- Monitor customer service performance against SLA commitments
- Track uptime, latency, throughput, and other key metrics
- Identify potential SLA breaches and take proactive action
- Generate SLA reports for customers and management
- Document SLA performance and exceptions
- Alert management to service degradation or issues

**Customer Support:**
- Respond to customer tickets, emails, and phone inquiries
- Provide technical support for customer deployments and services
- Troubleshoot connectivity, performance, and service issues
- Coordinate with infrastructure teams to resolve customer problems
- Communicate status updates during incidents or maintenance
- Build positive customer relationships through responsive service

**Monitoring & Alerting:**
- Monitor customer infrastructure using monitoring platforms
- Respond to alerts and anomalies affecting customer services
- Perform health checks on customer deployments
- Monitor network performance, server health, and application availability
- Escalate issues following defined procedures
- Maintain accuracy of monitoring configurations

**Incident Management:**
- Participate in incident response for customer-impacting events
- Document incidents and maintain incident records
- Coordinate communication with customers during service disruptions
- Support root cause analysis and post-incident reviews
- Track incident resolution and follow-up actions
- Ensure proper escalation of unresolved issues

**Service Delivery:**
- Support provisioning of new customer services
- Coordinate installation and turn-up activities
- Perform service testing and validation
- Assist with customer onboarding and orientation
- Manage customer access requests and permissions
- Support change requests and service modifications

**Reporting & Documentation:**
- Generate regular performance and availability reports
- Maintain customer service documentation
- Update knowledge base and troubleshooting guides
- Document customer configurations and requirements
- Create monthly reports for customer business reviews
- Track and report on key performance indicators

**Process Compliance:**
- Follow ITIL service management processes
- Maintain ticket quality and documentation standards
- Adhere to change management procedures
- Ensure proper escalation and communication protocols
- Participate in continuous improvement initiatives
- Support audit and compliance activities

**Customer Communication:**
- Provide proactive notification of planned maintenance
- Communicate service impacts and expected duration
- Respond promptly to customer inquiries
- Deliver clear, professional technical communication
- Manage customer expectations during issue resolution
- Build customer confidence through expertise and responsiveness

## Required Qualifications

- Associate's or Bachelor's degree in Information Technology or related field (or equivalent experience)
- Minimum 3 years experience in IT operations, support, or service delivery
- Understanding of data center services and infrastructure
- Good troubleshooting and problem-solving skills
- Familiarity with monitoring tools and ticketing systems
- Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)
- Good command of English and Georgian
- Customer service orientation and professionalism
- Ability to work shifts including evenings and weekends as needed

## Desired Qualifications

- ITIL Foundation certification
- Experience in data center or managed services environment
- Knowledge of networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, routing, switching)
- Familiarity with virtualization and cloud platforms
- Experience with monitoring platforms (Nagios, Zabbix, SolarWinds, PRTG)
- Previous customer-facing support role
- Technical certifications (CompTIA Network+, Server+, CCNA)
- Russian language proficiency

## Experience Level

**Mid-Level Professional** (3+ years experience)

**Note:** Multiple positions available. Some shift work may be required to provide 24/7 customer support coverage.
