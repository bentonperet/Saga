# GGE Job Descriptions
**Location: Tbilisi, Georgia**

## About the Facility

The Georgian Green Energy (GGE) complex in Tbilisi represents a groundbreaking integration of renewable energy generation and advanced digital infrastructure. This state-of-the-art facility combines a modern hydroelectric power plant with a cutting-edge data center, creating a unique ecosystem where sustainable energy production powers mission-critical digital operations.

Our Tbilisi Hydro Power Plant harnesses the natural flow of Georgia's water resources to generate clean, reliable electricity, while our collocated Data Center delivers world-class hosting and cloud services with industry-leading uptime and efficiency. This integrated approach positions GGE as a leader in sustainable digital infrastructure, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and operational excellence are not just compatible‚Äîthey're inseparable.

Both facilities operate under the highest standards of reliability, safety, and environmental stewardship. Every role at GGE is critical to maintaining the 24/7/365 operation of essential infrastructure that powers businesses and communities across the region.

## 1. CFO - Chief Financial Officer

**Location:** Tbilisi, Georgia

### Facility Introduction
Join Georgian Green Energy's leadership team at our integrated Tbilisi facility, where innovative hydroelectric generation meets state-of-the-art data center operations. This unique dual-facility environment requires sophisticated financial management across both renewable energy production and mission-critical digital infrastructure.

### Job Summary
The Chief Financial Officer will serve as a key member of the executive leadership team, responsible for all financial aspects of GGE's operations including the Hydro Power Plant and Data Center. This critical role ensures financial sustainability, regulatory compliance, and strategic financial planning to support the company's growth and operational excellence objectives.

### Reporting Structure
- **Reports to:** Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
- **Direct Reports:** Financial Controller, Accounting Manager, Financial Analysts, Procurement Manager
- **Key Relationships:** All C-suite executives, Board of Directors, external auditors, banking partners, regulatory authorities

### Roles & Responsibilities

**Strategic Financial Leadership:**
- Develop and execute comprehensive financial strategy aligned with company's sustainability and growth objectives
- Provide financial analysis and recommendations to CEO and Board of Directors
- Lead annual budgeting process for both HPP and Data Center operations
- Oversee capital allocation and investment decisions for facility expansions and upgrades

**Financial Operations Management:**
- Ensure accurate and timely financial reporting in compliance with Georgian and international standards
- Manage cash flow, working capital, and treasury operations
- Oversee accounts payable/receivable, payroll, and general ledger operations
- Establish and maintain robust internal controls and audit processes

**Energy & Data Center Financial Expertise:**
- Manage power purchase agreements (PPAs) and energy market transactions
- Develop pricing models for data center services and colocation offerings
- Monitor and optimize operational expenditure (OPEX) and capital expenditure (CAPEX)
- Track and report on sustainability metrics and ESG financial impacts

**Compliance & Risk Management:**
- Ensure compliance with Georgian tax regulations and international financial standards
- Manage relationships with external auditors, tax advisors, and regulatory bodies
- Develop and implement financial risk management strategies
- Oversee insurance programs for both facilities

**Banking & Investor Relations:**
- Maintain relationships with banking partners and financial institutions
- Manage debt facilities and refinancing activities
- Prepare materials for investors and potential financing partners
- Lead financial due diligence for strategic partnerships or transactions

### Required Qualifications
- Master's degree in Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or related field (MBA or CPA strongly preferred)
- Minimum 15 years progressive financial management experience
- At least 5 years in C-level financial leadership role
- Deep understanding of Georgian and international accounting standards (IFRS)
- Proven experience managing multi-million dollar budgets
- Strong knowledge of energy sector economics and renewable energy finance
- Excellent command of English and Georgian (written and spoken)
- Proficiency with enterprise financial systems (ERP) and business intelligence tools

### Desired Qualifications
- CPA, CFA, ACCA, or equivalent international finance certification
- Previous CFO experience in critical infrastructure, utilities, or data center sectors
- Experience with project finance and infrastructure development
- Knowledge of carbon markets and sustainability reporting frameworks
- Familiarity with data center economics and colocation business models

**Senior Executive** (15+ years experience, 5+ years C-level)
