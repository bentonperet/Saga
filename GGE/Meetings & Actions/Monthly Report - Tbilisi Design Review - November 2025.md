## Design Evolution - Capacity Enhancement

### AI and High-Density Computing Requirements

Based on market analysis and client requirements evolution, the design team identified the need to increase overall data center capacity to accommodate artificial intelligence and high-performance computing workloads.

**Design Modifications:**

**Electrical Systems**
- Increased electrical distribution capacity to support higher power densities
- Enhanced UPS and backup power systems for critical load growth
- Revised busway and panel board sizing for future flexibility
- Updated single line diagrams to reflect capacity increases

**Mechanical Systems**
- Enhanced cooling system capacity for higher heat rejection requirements
- Evaluated alternative cooling technologies for high-density applications (liquid cooling, rear-door heat exchangers)
- Increased chilled water plant capacity and redundancy
- Modified airflow management strategy for hot aisle containment

**Infrastructure Impact**
- Structural load considerations for denser equipment layouts
- Increased utility service requirements (power, water)
- Enhanced monitoring and control systems for thermal management
- Space planning adjustments to optimize power and cooling distribution
